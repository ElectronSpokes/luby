# Vision

## Problem

Health tracking apps are bloated, complicated, and dumb. They log data but don't understand it. You end up juggling five apps for food, water, movement, fasting, and coaching — none of them talk to each other, none of them know you.

Luby is different: one simple app with an AI brain that actually coaches you.

## Target Users

- Primary: 48k (personal tool, built for daily use)
- Secondary: anyone who wants a minimal, AI-coached wellness companion without the noise of mainstream health apps

## What Luby Is

A personal wellness companion that lives in your pocket. You point your camera at food, it tells you what's in it. You log water with a tap. You ask Luby for advice and it knows your day — what you ate, how you moved, where you're falling short. It coaches, it doesn't just count.

## Core Pillars

1. **Simple** — log anything in seconds, not minutes. No configuration hell.
2. **Smart** — AI that understands your day and gives specific, useful coaching. Not generic tips.
3. **Complete** — food, hydration, movement, fasting, meal planning, recipes — one app.
4. **Personal** — built for you. Your data, your patterns, your goals.

## Success Criteria

- Used daily (the real test — do you actually open it?)
- Food scanning works reliably with camera
- Coaching feels useful, not generic
- Available on phone (App Store / Google Play)
- Data persists and syncs across devices
- Works offline for basic logging, syncs when connected

## Non-Goals

- Social features / community (this is personal)
- Wearable integrations (future, not now)
- Calorie-obsessed diet culture (coaching, not shaming)
- Enterprise / team features
