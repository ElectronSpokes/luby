Luby is a simple, AI-coached wellness companion. One app for food logging (with camera-based scanning), hydration, movement, fasting sessions, meal plans, recipes, and conversational coaching.

**Pillars**

* Simple — log anything in seconds, not minutes
* Smart — AI that understands your day and gives specific coaching, not generic tips
* Complete — food, hydration, movement, fasting, meal planning, recipes in one app
* Personal — built for daily use; your data, your patterns

**What's inside**

* Camera-based food scanning (Gemini 2.5 Flash vision)
* Macro breakdowns (calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber, sugar)
* Hydration & movement quick-log
* Fasting timer with target durations
* Daily AI coaching plans tuned to your logged data
* 7-day AI meal plans with shopping lists
* Recipe search + save
* Conversational chat with the Luby coaching persona

**Distribution & privacy**

This build is distributed via the household F-Droid repo (signed by the household release keystore). No Google Play, no third-party update channel, no telemetry beyond what the API needs to function.
